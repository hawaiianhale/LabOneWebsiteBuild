The Kī Plant

Scientific Name:

Cordyline fruticosa

Common Name:

Ti Plant

Cultural Uses

Leaves are used for the following uses below:

  • Thatching for traditional hale
  • Food wrappers
  • Hula skirts
  • Sandals

The sweet roots were baked and eaten or used to make an alcoholic beverage called ‘okolehao.

How to Care for the Ki

Below are tips to mālama your ki plant

  1. Do not place plant in direct sunlight
  2. Do not water your ki plant everyday. Allow soil to dry during waterings
  3. Feed your ki plant food that does not contain fluoride
